AI Fundamentals 6 questions
Artificial intelligence is software that learns from patterns in data to make decisions, generate content, understand language, and solve problems — without needing explicit step-by-step programming for every task. In 2026, AI means tools you talk to in plain English that write, analyse, automate, and create on your behalf.
Generative AI creates new content — text, images, audio, video, code — from a prompt. Tools like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Midjourney, and ElevenLabs are all generative AI. They don't just retrieve information; they produce original outputs based on your instructions.
A large language model is the engine behind AI chatbots like ChatGPT and Claude. It's trained on vast amounts of text and learns statistical patterns in language — allowing it to predict and generate coherent, context-aware text in response to any prompt.
AI is the broad field of making machines intelligent. Machine learning is a subset where models learn from data. Deep learning is a subset of machine learning using neural networks with many layers — it powers most modern AI including ChatGPT, image generators, and Voice AI. Think of them as nested circles: AI contains ML, which contains deep learning.
AI will not take your job. A person who knows how to use AI effectively will. The professionals thriving in 2026 are those who treat AI as a 10x productivity multiplier — not those who ignore it. The greatest career risk right now is not learning AI at all. This academy exists precisely to close that gap.
Start with the three tools you will use every day: ChatGPT for quick tasks and content, Claude for deep analysis and long documents, and Gemini for Google Workspace integration. Then layer in automation, Voice AI, and building. That is exactly the progression inside this academy — beginning with Module 01 and building systematically from there.

ChatGPT 6 questions
ChatGPT is an AI assistant by OpenAI that can write content, answer questions, analyse documents, generate images, browse the web, run code, and build Custom GPTs. You interact with it entirely in plain English — no technical skills required. It is the most widely used AI tool in the world.
ChatGPT is used for writing emails, blog posts, social media and marketing copy; summarising documents and research; answering customer queries; generating business plans; coding and debugging; image creation; data analysis; and building AI-powered products. In business, it functions as a research analyst, copywriter, customer service agent, and strategist in one tool.
Yes — ChatGPT has a free tier giving access to GPT-4o mini. The paid tier (ChatGPT Plus) at approximately £20/month unlocks GPT-4o with advanced capabilities: real-time web browsing, image generation via DALL-E, file uploads, voice mode, and access to Custom GPTs. For business use the paid tier is recommended and delivers exceptional ROI.
A Custom GPT is a version of ChatGPT you personalise with your own instructions, knowledge base, and personality — no coding required. You can build a Custom GPT that knows your business, speaks in your brand voice, and handles specific tasks automatically. Module 07 of the academy teaches this step by step.
Prompt engineering is the skill of writing instructions to AI that produce high-quality, accurate, useful outputs. The same AI tool gives drastically different results depending on how you phrase your request. A well-engineered prompt specifies the role, task, format, tone, constraints, and context. This single skill multiplies the value of every AI tool you use — covered in depth in Module 01.
GPT-4o (the "o" stands for omni) is OpenAI's flagship model. It processes text, images, audio, and documents in a single session and responds significantly faster than previous versions. It is the model behind ChatGPT Plus and remains one of the most capable general-purpose AI models available in 2026.

Claude AI 5 questions
Claude is an AI assistant built by Anthropic. It is known for its ability to handle extremely long documents (up to 200,000 tokens in a single context), its natural and nuanced writing quality, and its careful step-by-step reasoning. It is widely considered the best AI for analysis, long-form writing, and sensitive communications.
Claude excels at long documents, nuanced reasoning, and human-sounding writing. ChatGPT is faster for quick tasks, has better image generation, and a larger ecosystem of Custom GPTs. Claude is safer and less prone to hallucination on analytical tasks. Both are covered in the academy and you will learn exactly when to deploy each one.
Yes — Claude has a free tier. The Claude Pro plan at approximately £20/month unlocks the most capable models, longer context windows, priority access, and more messages per day. The free tier is sufficient for getting started; Pro is recommended for heavy business use.
Claude is best for: summarising and analysing long contracts, reports, and research papers; writing long-form proposals, pitches, and articles that read as naturally human; handling nuanced or sensitive communications; and complex multi-step reasoning tasks where accuracy and careful thinking matter more than speed.
Claude was created by Anthropic, an AI safety company founded in 2021 by former OpenAI researchers including Dario Amodei and Daniela Amodei. Anthropic's focus on AI safety is reflected in Claude's measured, careful approach. Claude is built on what Anthropic calls Constitutional AI — a framework for making AI models more honest and harmless.

Google Gemini 4 questions

Gemini is integrated natively into Gmail, Google Docs, Sheets, Slides, and Drive — making it the most practical AI tool for people already working inside Google Workspace.

AI Agents & Automation 5 questions
An AI agent is an AI system that can plan, decide, and take multi-step actions autonomously to complete a goal — without a human confirming every step. Unlike a chatbot that responds to one question at a time, an agent can research a topic, write a report, format it, and send it — all in sequence, without interruption. Module 07 covers building and deploying AI agents.
Agentic AI refers to AI systems that operate with autonomy — they receive a high-level goal and independently determine the steps, tools, and decisions needed to achieve it. This is the frontier of AI in 2026. Rather than answering a single question, agentic AI manages entire workflows, makes decisions, and executes complex multi-step tasks end to end.
AI automation connects AI models to your existing tools and data, creating workflows that run without manual input. For example: a new lead fills in a form → AI qualifies them → personalised email is drafted and sent → CRM is updated → follow-up is scheduled. All of this happens with zero human involvement. Tools like Make, Zapier, and n8n power these workflows and are covered in Module 04.
A flow agent is an AI agent that operates within a defined workflow or pipeline — executing a series of connected steps in sequence or in parallel to complete a business process. Flow agents are used for sales pipelines, content production workflows, data processing, and customer service escalation paths. Module 07 teaches you to build and deploy flow agents from scratch.
A Voice AI Agent makes and receives real phone calls and holds natural, conversational dialogues — answering questions, booking appointments, qualifying leads, and handling customer service. It sounds human, never sleeps, and can handle hundreds of simultaneous calls. Tools like VAPI, Bland AI, and Retell AI make this possible without deep technical knowledge.
